What is NerdHub?

Greetings and salutations! Here at the Hub, we strive to educate people who may be on the fence about all things nerd. Maybe it's dipping a toe into the world of D&D (Dungeons & Dragons), building that first computer to play the newest game with your friends, or maybe you just want to collect them all in the Pokémon TCG (Trading Card Game).

We hope that what you find here leads you or a loved one to that wonderful world of high-fantasy or sci-fi you never knew existed. You can find a plethora of information by following the links in the navigation menu above. While we do our best to provide as much knowledge in one place, the world of nerddom is vast — so we have provided useful links throughout to friendly websites covering your desired topic. With all that said, welcome to the world of NerdHub!
